Frequently asked questions

Do you plan do add more books? When?

The plan is to support up to book 12, and no more. Books 11 and 12, probably, will be available for summer 2018.

How many books are there? Why are not all books supported?

The Project AON, up to March 2018, has published (I think) 28 books from the Lone Wolf serie in English, and 13 in Spanish. The work we do with this application is to support the game rules. These rules must to be customized for EACH section of EACH book. It's not a simple work. You have to read the whole book, and program the rules.

Is this game original?

No, at all. This application is an adaptation of Lone Wolf game books, to play them on Android or a web browser. We have not written or translated the books, and we don't have any right over them. Books were written by Joe Dever, and translated by Project AON volunteers.

You can download the books from the Project AON web. Also, there are other tools and adaptations to play the books.

I have found a bug

You can report bugs on these places:

  • In Google Play Reviews (not the best place)
  • English forum
  • Spanish forum
  • Development site
We cannot promise we will fix it, but we will try it. Our native language is not English, so we are specially interested on grammatical errors on the application messages (not in the book text itself).
